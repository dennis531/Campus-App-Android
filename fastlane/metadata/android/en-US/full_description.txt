The Campus App is based on the TUM Campus App. It can be used on both smartphones and tablets and offers the following features to help you through your daily university life:
- Lecture appointments
- News from campus
- Information about events
- Cafeteria menus
- departure times
- Campus maps
- Find study rooms
... and more!

The app is intended for both students and employees!

Support: https://github.com/dennis531/Campus-App-Android / campus-app@uos.de

Disclaimer: This is not an official app of a university and there is consequentially no official support or liability.

Permissions and what they are used for:
- Location services: so we can show you nearest Cafeteria / departures
- Calendar access: to sync your lecture calendar with your local calendar
- Contacts: so you can add people from the person search to your address book
- Detailed WiFi information: so we can help you set up eduroam access
- Camera: so you can join chat rooms by scanning a QR code and add images to the feedback form
- Storage: to provide offline access and faster loading
- Disable sleep mode: to receive messages when the display is turned off
